Our Mission

We are a team of mainly unemployed space enthusiasts, coders, and data explorers participating in the NASA SpaceApps Challenge 2025. Our goal is to make exoplanet detection more accessible and engaging for everyone through interactive visualizations and to find other exoplings like us.
